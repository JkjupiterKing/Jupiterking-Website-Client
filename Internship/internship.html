How to Apply

  1. Review the internship technologies and ensure your skills align.
  2. Prepare your resume and cover letter highlighting relevant experience.
  3. Send your application to info@jupiterkingtechnologies.com with subject "Internship Application".
  4. Complete an initial phone screening interview.
  5. Attend technical interviews and/or assessments as scheduled.
  6. Receive offer and onboarding details.
